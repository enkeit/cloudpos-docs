# Billing & Checkout

The billing screen is the core of the POS. It is split into three panels:

- **Left** — Product catalog and category browser
- **Center** — Active cart / order items
- **Right** — Payment summary and actions

## Adding Products to Cart

1. Browse categories in the left panel or use the search bar.
2. Tap a product to add it to the cart.
3. Tap the cart item to adjust quantity, price, or apply a discount.

## Applying Discounts & Coupons

- Tap **Discount** on the price summary to enter a flat or percentage discount.
- To apply a coupon, tap **Coupon** and enter or scan the coupon code.

## Selecting a Customer

Tap the **Customer** field at the top of the cart to search and attach a customer. This enables loyalty points and saves purchase history.

## Payment

1. Tap **Pay** to open the payment modal.
2. Select a payment method (Cash, Card, UPI, etc.).
3. Enter the amount received and tap **Confirm**.
4. A receipt will be printed or sent via WhatsApp.

## Delivery Methods

For businesses with delivery, select a **Delivery Method** from the modal before completing payment. This logs the delivery address and method on the order.

## Restaurant Mode

In restaurant mode, the billing screen shows a **Tables Panel**. Select a table, take an order per seat, and send it to the kitchen as a KOT before final checkout.

## Pending / Saved Orders

Incomplete orders are saved as pending. Access them from the **Orders** tab in the bottom bar (mobile) or the **Pending Orders** sidebar (desktop).
