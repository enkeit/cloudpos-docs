# Kiosk Mode

Kiosk Mode turns the device into a self-service ordering terminal for customers.

## Enabling Kiosk Mode

Kiosk Mode is activated from the Admin Panel under **Store Settings → Kiosk**. Once enabled, staff can launch it from the POS app sidebar.

## How Kiosk Works

1. Customer browses the product catalog on the kiosk screen.
2. They add items to their cart and proceed to checkout.
3. Payment is processed (cash or card, depending on configuration).
4. A receipt is printed and the order appears in the kitchen queue.

## FAQ & Policy Pages

The kiosk includes built-in pages for:
- **FAQ** — Common customer questions
- **Privacy Policy**
- **Terms & Conditions**

These pages are editable from the Admin Panel.

## Exiting Kiosk Mode

To exit kiosk mode (staff only), tap the hidden exit button and enter the staff PIN.
