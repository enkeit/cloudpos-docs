# Customers

## Adding a Customer

1. Go to **Customers** from the sidebar.
2. Tap **Add Customer** and fill in the name, phone, and optional address.
3. Save. The customer is now searchable from the billing screen.

## Customer Profile

Tap a customer to open their profile, which shows:

- **Information** — Name, phone, email, address
- **Orders** — Full purchase history
- **Transactions** — Payments and account balance
- **Loyalty Points** — Earned and redeemed points
- **Account Book** — Credit/debit ledger if the customer has a running account

## Loyalty Program

Loyalty points are awarded based on rules configured in the Admin Panel. Points can be redeemed as a discount at checkout.

## Customer Vouchers

If a customer has vouchers, they appear in the **Vouchers** tab of their profile and can be applied during billing.
