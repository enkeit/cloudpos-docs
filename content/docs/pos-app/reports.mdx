# Reports

Reports are accessible from the sidebar. All reports support date range filtering.

## Sales Report

Shows total sales, returns, and net revenue for the selected period. Filter by:
- Date range (daily, weekly, custom)
- Payment method
- Staff member

## Product Sales Report

Breakdown of quantity sold and revenue per product. Useful for identifying top-selling items.

## Stock Report

Current stock levels for all products. Highlights low-stock items.

## Consumed Stock Report

Shows raw material or ingredient consumption (relevant for restaurants using recipe management).

## Non-Stock Report

Lists items sold that are not tracked in inventory.

## Supplier Sales Report

Purchase summaries grouped by supplier.

## Daily Close

At end of day, use **Daily Close** to:
1. Reconcile cash in drawer vs. system total
2. Print a close-of-day summary receipt
3. Lock the session for the day

The daily close report is printable on both thermal and standard printers.
