# Products & Categories

## Browsing Products

Products are grouped by categories. On the home/billing screen, tap a category chip to filter the product grid. Use the search bar to find a product by name or barcode scan.

## Product Details

Each product can have:
- **Name & Description**
- **Price** — Base selling price
- **Unit** — e.g. kg, pcs, litre
- **Stock** — Available quantity (if stock tracking is enabled)
- **Category** — For filtering on the POS
- **Barcode** — For scan-to-add

## Adding Products (POS App)

Product creation and editing is done in the **Admin Panel**. The POS app syncs products from the server. If a product is not showing, pull to refresh or check your internet connection.

## Categories

Categories are managed from the Admin Panel. In the POS app, categories appear as tabs or filter chips on the product grid.

## Stock Alerts

If a product's stock falls below the minimum threshold, the app will show a warning before adding it to the cart (configurable in Admin settings).
