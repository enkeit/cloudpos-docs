# Admin Panel Overview

The **Enke POS Admin Panel** is the web-based back-office for managing your business. It is built with Laravel and accessible from any browser.

## What You Can Manage

- **Products & Categories** — Add, edit, and organise your product catalog
- **Users & Roles** — Create staff accounts and define permissions
- **Customers** — View and manage customer database
- **Purchases** — Record supplier purchases and manage stock
- **Reports** — Detailed sales, financial, and inventory analytics
- **Settings** — Store configuration, tax rules, payment methods, receipt layouts

## Accessing the Admin Panel

Open a browser and go to your store's admin URL (provided by your IT team). Log in with your admin credentials.

## User Roles

The admin panel supports multiple roles. Each role has specific permissions. See [Users & Roles](/docs/admin-panel/users-roles) for details.
