# Reports

The Admin Panel provides detailed reports for finance, sales, and inventory.

## Sales Report

Full breakdown of all invoices:
- Filter by date range, store, staff, or payment method
- Exportable as CSV or PDF

## Financial Reports

- **Transaction Report** — All financial transactions
- **Invoice Account Report** — Summary by account type
- **Voucher Account Report** — Payment voucher breakdown

## Inventory Reports

- **Stock Report** — Current stock levels per product
- **Consumed Stock Report** — Ingredient/raw material usage
- **Non-Stock Report** — Sales of non-tracked items

## Supplier Reports

- **Supplier Sales Report** — Purchase totals per supplier
- **Outstanding Payables** — Unpaid supplier balances

## Customer Reports

- **Customer Purchase History** — Per-customer spend and frequency
- **Customer Account Book** — Credit balance and transaction history

## Dashboard Analytics

The admin dashboard shows:
- Revenue charts (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Top products
- Store comparison (multi-branch)
- Low stock alerts

## Exporting Reports

Every report has an **Export** button. Formats supported: CSV, PDF.
