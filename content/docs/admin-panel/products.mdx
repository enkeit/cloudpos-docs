# Products & Categories

## Adding a Product

1. Go to **Products → Add Product**.
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Name** — Product display name
   - **Category** — Select or create a category
   - **Price** — Selling price
   - **Unit** — Unit of measurement (pcs, kg, litre, etc.)
   - **Barcode** — Optional; can be scanned at POS
   - **Stock Quantity** — Starting stock (if tracking is enabled)
3. Click **Save**.

## Bulk Import

Use the **Import** button on the Products list page to upload a CSV file. Download the template first to get the correct column format.

## Managing Categories

Go to **Products → Categories** to create, edit, or reorder categories. Categories appear as filter tabs on the POS app home screen.

## Product Variants

If a product comes in multiple sizes/colours, enable **Variants** on the product and add each variant with its own price and stock.

## Stock Management

Enable **Stock Tracking** per product. Once enabled:
- Stock decreases automatically on each sale
- You can set a **Minimum Stock** alert threshold
- Manual stock adjustments are logged under **Inventory**
