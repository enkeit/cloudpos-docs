# Customer Management

## Customer List

Go to **Customers** to see all registered customers. You can search by name, phone, or email, and export the list as CSV.

## Customer Details

Click a customer to view:
- Contact details and addresses
- Complete purchase history
- Loyalty point balance
- Account book (if credit account is enabled)

## Loyalty Program Configuration

Go to **Settings → Loyalty** to configure:
- Points earned per currency unit spent
- Redemption rate (e.g. 100 points = $1 discount)
- Expiry period for points

## Vouchers

Create vouchers under **Customers → Vouchers**. Assign them to specific customers or make them general-use. Vouchers can be single-use or multi-use with a value or percentage discount.

## Customer Account Book

Customers can have a running credit balance (account book). Enable this per customer from their profile. The account book tracks all credits, debits, and outstanding balances.
